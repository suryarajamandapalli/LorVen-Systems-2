*   **Division Slug:** `snt`
*   **Division Title:** Signalling & Telecom
*   **Category:** SIGNALLING & TELECOM
*   **Hero Title:** RDPMS
*   **Hero Description:** Remote Diagnostic & Predictive Maintenance System for wayside track and signalling asset health monitoring.
*   **Introduction:** Continuous predictive monitoring of safety-critical wayside assets.
    *   *Paragraph 1:* The Remote Diagnostic & Predictive Maintenance System (RDPMS) is an IoT-based telemetry platform designed to monitor the operational health of railway signaling gears. The wayside remote terminal unit (RTU) intercepts analog current signatures, voltage thresholds, and contact resistance levels without interfering with vital interlocking logic circuits.
    *   *Paragraph 2:* Data is collected at 100Hz sampling rates and transmitted over redundant dual-SIM LTE cellular routes to a centralized monitoring server. By comparing live telemetry against historical baseline curves, the system flags mechanical wear, degradation, and electrical anomalies weeks before a wayside asset failure occurs.
*   **Features:**
    *   *100Hz Signal Sampling:* Captures high-resolution electrical waveforms from point machine motors to detect friction increases and slide degradation.
    *   *Galvanic Isolation:* Sensing input channels are opto-isolated up to 2.5kV to prevent wayside surge propagation and guarantee no logical interference with interlocking circuits.
    *   *Insulation Leakage Tracking:* Monitors insulation resistance to ground on signaling cables, warning of degradation before short circuits shut down the line section.
    *   *Redundant Telemetry:* Equipped with dual-SIM LTE routing that falls back to GSM-R or GPRS protocols if primary network connectivity is lost.
    *   *Edge Logging Core:* Internal flash storage preserves up to 1 million diagnostic events locally to prevent data loss during network dropouts.
    *   *Environmental Supervision:* Supervises wayside cabin ambient parameters including temperature, humidity, and door status for physical security.
*   **Technical Specifications:**
    *   *Analog Inputs:* 16 opto-isolated channels, 12-bit analog-to-digital resolution
    *   *Digital Inputs:* 32 opto-isolated channels, event tracking resolution < 10ms
    *   *Sampling Frequency:* 100 samples/second per analog channel simultaneously
    *   *Operating Voltage:* 24V DC / 110V AC wayside supply options
    *   *Communication Port:* Dual redundant SIM card slots, 4G LTE with GPRS fallback
    *   *Standards Compliance:* EN 50155 (electronic equipment on rolling stock), SIL-2 assessed

## 2. IFD

*   **Slug:** `ifd`
*   **Division Slug:** `snt`
*   **Division Title:** Signalling & Telecom
*   **Category:** SIGNALLING & TELECOM
*   **Hero Title:** IFD
*   **Hero Description:** Online Insulation Failure Detector system for continuous real-time monitoring of wayside signaling circuit insulation resistance.
*   **Introduction:** Automated insulation resistance logging for safety-critical railway cables.
    *   *Paragraph 1:* The Insulation Failure Detector (IFD) is an online monitoring system designed to supervise the insulation resistance (IR) of signaling cables and point machine conductors relative to earth. Cable insulation decay due to moisture ingress or mechanical wear is a primary cause of ground faults and unsafe signal state changes.
    *   *Paragraph 2:* The system injects a non-disruptive, low-voltage diagnostic signal into the lines under load, measuring insulation levels from 10kΩ to 10MΩ without interfering with active signaling commands. Degradation alerts are immediately logged, enabling proactive maintenance before ground faults occur.
*   **Features:**
    *   *Sequential Scanning Core:* Sequentially scans up to 96 signaling circuits to supervise conductor insulation values from a single terminal unit.
    *   *Live Circuit Verification:* Conducts insulation measurements under live operating conditions without disrupting safety-critical signaling commands.
    *   *Opto-Isolated Measurement:* Measurement channels are galvanically isolated up to 2.5kV to protect signal lines from high-voltage surges.
    *   *RS-485 Modbus Telemetry:* Transmits real-time measurements and alarm triggers to station loggers via RS-485 Modbus or Ethernet channels.
    *   *Local Status Control Panel:* Onboard console permits channel configuration, threshold adjustments, and real-time resistance lookups.
    *   *Conductor Current Logging:* Archives historical leakage current trends to trace conductor insulation decay curves over months.
*   **Technical Specifications:**
    *   *Measurement Range:* 10 kΩ to 10 MΩ with ±5% measurement accuracy
    *   *Channel Capacity:* Up to 96 independent measurement channels per unit
    *   *Test Signal Voltage:* < 24V DC test signal to avoid circuit interference
    *   *Operating Supply:* 110V AC or 24V DC station power options
    *   *Telemetry Port:* RS-485 Modbus RTU / Ethernet interfaces
